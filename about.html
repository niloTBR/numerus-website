About Numerus

A legacy of excellence spanning generations

WHO WE ARE

Numerus Group is a family-owned group of partner companies operating across multiple strategic sectors including logistics, oil & gas services, water treatment, and automotive. Our principles bring generations of business experience, built on a foundation of integrity, diligence, and an unwavering commitment to fairness.

With a direct workforce of more than 200 trained professionals and an extensive network of regional partners, Numerus combines local execution capability with international standards and practices.
