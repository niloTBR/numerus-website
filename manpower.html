MANPOWER SUPPLY

Al-Gharraf Oil Services partners with Indman, a global manpower specialist with over 30 years of experience, 189+ international clients, and more than 107,000 deployed specialists across managerial, technical, non-technical, and administrative roles. Together, we deliver end-to-end workforce solutions tailored to complex project environments in Iraq and worldwide.

Recruitment Services

  • Reviewing job descriptions and finalizing terms & conditions
  • Searching through global databases, job portals, headhunting, and social media
  • Screening, shortlisting, and securing signed job offers
  • Managing medicals, compliance, and deployment schedules
  • Coordinating client approvals and mobilization timelines

Secondment & Contract Hiring

  • Matching job profiles with global talent pools
  • Mobilizing personnel from active worldwide databases
  • Arranging interviews, evaluations, and trade tests
  • Managing contracts, visas, medicals, and onboarding
  • Ensuring compliance with local labour laws and client standards

Logistics & Global Mobility

  • Air, road, and hotel arrangements
  • Assistance with medicals, training centres, consulates, and airports
  • Delivery of original travel documents to the airport when needed

Technical Training & Competency Testing

  • Simulated work environments
  • Tools and machinery for hands-on evaluation
  • Testing under client supervision or certified technical evaluators
  • Compliance with industry-specific QC norms

Visa Processing & Payroll

  • Visa processing, residency permits, and security passes
  • Payroll management under third-party or direct-hire arrangements
  • Compliance with local regulations and client requirements

Disciplines We Supply

  • Engineering: Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Instrumentation, Process
  • Oilfield & Drilling: Rig crews, well services, production operations
  • Construction & EPC: Supervisors, foremen, welders, fabricators, technicians
  • HSE & QA/QC: Safety officers, inspectors, auditors
  • Administrative & Support: Document controllers, logistics coordinators, camp staff
