Logistics

Nationwide express, cargo and ground transportation solutions

ABOUT

Numerus Group operates one of Iraq's most established logistics networks through Al-Estilam Express and previously the Iraqi Transportation Network (ITN). For over two decades, we have delivered reliable, secure and fully compliant logistics services to multinational corporations, government entities and commercial clients across the country.

Our logistics operations combine international standards with deep local expertise, enabling seamless movement of goods across Iraq and the wider region.

IRAQI TRANSPORTATION NETWORK (ITN)

Ground transportation at scale

ITN, originally established as Al-Saqir Express Cargo (ASEC) in 2008, pioneered secure commercial ground transportation between Jordan, Kuwait and Iraq, and within Iraq itself. The company was later integrated into Al-Estilam Express, strengthening Numerus Group's overland logistics capability.

KEY STRENGTHS

24/7 operations across regional corridors and intra-Iraq routes

1,600+ vetted drivers and trucks, including flatbeds, lowboys and fuel tankers

Secure hubs in Ramadi and Safwan with trained personnel and material-handling equipment

Prime contractor for the U.S. Government, meeting stringent quality and security standards

Over 200,000 missions executed with a 95% Required Delivery Date (RDD) success rate

Strong commitment to the Iraqi First philosophy, supporting local employment and economic development

ITN's scale, reliability and security record make it a trusted partner for high-value, time-critical and sensitive cargo movements.
