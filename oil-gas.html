ABOUT AGOS

Al-Gharraf Oil Services Co. Ltd. (AGOS), a Numerus Group company, provides integrated support services to international energy operators and infrastructure developers across Iraq. With over 15 years of continuous operations, AGOS combines deep local execution capability with international operating standards to deliver reliable, compliant, and scalable solutions in some of Iraq's most demanding environments.

FLAGSHIP PROJECT

Al-Hartha Water Treatment Plant, Basra

AGOS operates under a five-year subcontract with Veolia-OTV to provide full O&M services at the largest pretreatment and reverse osmosis drinking-water plant in Basra.

  • 300,000 m³/day design capacity
  • Day-to-day operational management
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Supply of essential treatment chemicals

Designed and constructed by Veolia-OTV in joint venture with Hitachi. Commissioned in 2022.
