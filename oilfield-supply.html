OILFIELD SUPPLY

Al-Gharraf Oil Services provides a complete portfolio of valves, pipes, fittings, drilling equipment, and industrial materials essential for upstream, midstream, and downstream operations. Through our affiliation with Oil World Oilfield Service Company, we deliver high-quality products backed by engineering expertise, certified manufacturing partners, and rapid delivery across Iraq and the region.

Valves & Actuation Systems

  • Gate, Globe, Ball, Plug, Butterfly, Needle & Check Valves
  • Relief & Safety Valves
  • Reducing Valves and Diaphragm Valves
  • Electro-Hydraulic (E-H) Actuator Systems
  • Pneumatic & Hydraulic Actuators
  • On/Off and Modulating Control Systems
  • Fail-Safe and Extreme-Service Actuators

Pipes, Fittings & Flanges

  • SMLS, SAW, ERW pipes (1/8" to 48" ODs)
  • DRL & SRL lengths, Standard to XXS wall thickness
  • LR & SR Elbows, Equal & Reducing Tees
  • Concentric & Eccentric Reducers, Caps, Laterals
  • Spiral Wound, Metal Ring, and Asbestos-free Soft Cut Gaskets (ASME/ANSI/API)

Drilling & Production Equipment

  • Mud Pumps, Frac Pumps, Triplex Pumps
  • Well Service Pumps, Hydrostatic Testing Pumps
  • Adjustable & Positive Chokes
  • Surface Safety Valves, Flow Heads
  • Choke, Kill & Cement Manifolds
  • Standpipe & Buffer Manifolds

Instrumentation & Measurement

  • Tubing & Fittings
  • Flow, Level, Temperature & Pressure Instruments
  • Gas Detectors (Portable & Fixed)
  • Smart Transmitters
  • Calibration Instruments
  • Sight Flow Indicators

Electrical Systems

  • Panel Boards, Circuit Breakers & Safety Switches
  • Electrical Test Equipment
  • Heat Tracing Systems
  • Cable Glands & Management Systems
  • Power, Control & Telecom Cables
  • Junction Boxes & Lighting for Hazardous Areas

Manufacturing & Engineering

  • Chemical Injection Packages, Process Skids
  • Pressure Vessels & Knock-Out Drums
  • Separators & Columns, Heat Exchangers
  • Flare Systems & Thermal Oxidizers
  • Pig Launchers & Receivers
  • Metering & HIPPS Skids
  • Certified: ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ASME U/U2/S/R, API 6A

Our Presence

  • Iraq: Baghdad, Basra, Erbil
  • UAE: Dubai

Operations across all major Iraqi governorates, supported by a workforce of 250+ professionals.
