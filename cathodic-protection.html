CATHODIC PROTECTION

Al-Gharraf Oil Services partners with Cumberland Cathodic Protection Ltd. (CCPL) — a global pioneer in cathodic protection since 1912, when Commander Elliot Cumberland developed the world's first commercial impressed-current corrosion prevention system. Today, CCPL delivers advanced corrosion-control solutions for onshore and offshore assets across power, oil & gas, marine, and industrial sectors.

Survey & Assessment

  • Electrolyte resistivity surveys
  • Soil/electrolyte analysis
  • Interaction and location surveys
  • Pearson, electromagnetic coating attenuation & close-interval potential surveys

Design

  • Sacrificial anode systems
  • Impressed current systems
  • Upgrading of existing CP systems
  • CAD-based engineering

Installation & Commissioning

  • Full installation and supervision
  • System commissioning and setting-to-work
  • Interaction testing
  • Permanent operation setup

Monitoring & Maintenance

  • Routine inspection and performance monitoring
  • Remote monitoring
  • Defect detection and troubleshooting

Applications

  • Cooling water systems, pumps, screens, filters, strainers
  • Heat exchangers & condenser water boxes
  • Jetties, tubular piles, sheet piles, intake pipes, subsea pipelines
  • Onshore & offshore pipelines
  • Storage tanks (internal & external systems)
  • Concrete structures with steel reinforcement

Equipment Supply

  • Transformer rectifiers (air-cooled and oil-cooled, manual or automatic)
  • Platinised titanium, MMO ribbon, mesh & tubular anodes
  • Silicon iron anodes
  • Aluminium, zinc, and magnesium sacrificial anodes
  • Junction and bond boxes (NEMA 4/4X and NEMA 7/8/9 explosion-proof)
