EPC & CAMP CONSTRUCTION

Al-Gharraf Oil Services (AGOS) delivers fully integrated EPC services tailored to the demanding requirements of Iraq's oil & gas sector. With more than a decade of experience supporting international operators, AGOS provides end-to-end project execution — from design and procurement to construction, commissioning, and handover. Our EPC division is led by industry veterans Mr. Mohammed Shanshal and Ali Mahmoud, each bringing over 30 years of engineering and construction experience.

Engineering Capability

  • Civil, electrical, and mechanical design
  • Construction engineering and site planning
  • Specialized engineering through partners such as Axia International for advanced or high-precision requirements

Procurement & Logistics Strength

  • Basra-based procurement for southern Iraq
  • Baghdad office for major Iraqi suppliers
  • Dubai office for international vendors
  • Local construction teams for on-ground purchasing
  • 50–100 containers per month via Numerus Group logistics

Construction Capabilities

  • Full project management
  • Camp construction (accommodation, offices, kitchens, clinics, utilities)
  • Civil works: warehouses, roads, security systems, well pads, earthworks
  • Electro-mechanical installations
  • Calibration & instrumentation through certified subcontractors

QHSE Commitment

  • ISO 9001 – Quality Management
  • ISO 14001 – Environmental Management
  • ISO 45001 – Occupational Health & Safety
  • HACCP / ISO 22000 – Food Safety

Document Control

  • MDR/VDR development
  • Submittals for review and approval
  • Revision control with CRS
  • AFC and As-Built documentation
  • Complete MRB, SPIR, and IOM packages

Completed Projects

  • Petrofac – 300-man camp, Majnoon (CPF-2)
  • NPS – 200-person base camp, Barjasia
  • Constellis – DS6 dog kennel facility (ExxonMobil)
  • Kitchen & dining facility for Petrofac (North Rumaila)
  • Warehouses and workshops in North Rumaila
  • Multiple DFAC installations supporting life support operations

Our Presence

  • Iraq: Baghdad, Basra, Erbil
  • UAE: Dubai

Operations across all major Iraqi governorates, supported by a workforce of 250+ professionals.
