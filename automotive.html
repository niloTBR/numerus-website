ABOUT

Leading Star, a subsidiary of Numerus Group, delivers premium commercial vehicle solutions to the Iraqi market, supporting the country's growing logistics, construction, oil & gas, and infrastructure sectors. Leveraging global engineering standards and Numerus Group's nationwide operational footprint, Leading Star provides reliable, scalable, and market-specific fleet solutions.
