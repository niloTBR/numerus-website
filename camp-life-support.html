CAMP LIFE SUPPORT

Al-Gharraf Oil Services (AGOS) delivers fully integrated Camp Life Support solutions designed for the demanding environments of Iraq's oil & gas fields. With more than a decade of continuous operations across Basra, Rumaila, Zubair, West Qurna, Badra, and other strategic locations, AGOS provides reliable, high-quality services that keep remote camps running safely, efficiently, and comfortably.

Catering Excellence

  • Daily production of high-quality, hygienic meals
  • Multi-national menu planning for diverse workforces
  • Fresh bakery and pastry production
  • Strict food safety and HACCP-compliant processes
  • Reliable supply chain for fresh, chilled, and frozen products

Housekeeping, Laundry & Camp Hygiene

  • Daily room cleaning and sanitation
  • Laundry services for residents and operations
  • Janitorial services for offices, kitchens, and common areas
  • Rapid response to resident requests and maintenance needs

Maintenance & Facility Support

  • Preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Electrical, mechanical, HVAC, and plumbing services
  • Generator and utility system support
  • Kitchen and laundry equipment maintenance
  • Full design, procurement, and installation of kitchen and laundry equipment

Waste Management & Environmental Compliance

  • Safe collection and disposal
  • Segregation and environmental compliance
  • Clean, hazard-free camp environments

Supply Chain & Inventory

  • FedEx operations in Iraq
  • Al-Awsat importing 50–60 containers per month
  • ITN transportation capabilities
  • Minimum two-week stock of frozen/dry goods
  • 2–3 days of fresh produce
  • Custom web-based purchasing system for requisitions, approvals, and tracking

Proven Track Record

  • Baker Hughes – 400–600 residents, multiple rigs
  • Petrofac – Base camp O&M and catering
  • Egyptian Maintenance Co. – 400+ residents
  • ZPEC – 5–8 camps across Badra and Nasiriyah
  • ICTSI – Umm Qasr port operations
  • ERIELL – Multiple rigs in WQ-2
  • Halliburton, Nabors, MUSC, NPS, Honghua, and others

Our Presence

  • Iraq: Baghdad, Basra, Erbil
  • UAE: Dubai

Operations across all major Iraqi governorates, supported by a workforce of 250+ professionals.
