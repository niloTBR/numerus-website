FIVE DECADES OF PARTNERSHIP

For over five decades, Numerus Group has delivered complex, high-impact projects across Iraq's most critical sectors, partnering with leading global multinationals to drive sustainable growth and operational excellence. Our track record spans oil & gas, power generation, logistics, FMCG distribution, and large-scale infrastructure — executed with integrity, precision, and an unwavering commitment to value creation.

From securing and operating major energy facilities to building nationwide logistics networks and introducing world-class consumer brands, our projects reflect deep market knowledge, proven capability, and a legacy of trust. Each initiative reinforces our mission: enabling progress and shaping Iraq's economic future.
